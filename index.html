Collector Onslaught

The collectors, an ancient alien race, are sending their hordes to fight you. You're in charge of the SSV Normandy SR-2, a spaceship armed with the latest in Alliance weaponry, and you're going to need to fend them off for as long as you can.

NOTE: It's highly recommended you use fullscreen while playing the game for the optimal experience. (Press F11 )

Controls:

Use the arrows keys ← ↑ → ↓ to move the targeting system and press Spacebar to fire.

Press 1 to switch to your Thanix single-fire cannon. Each tap of the Spacebar will fire a new shot. The Thanix cannon is semi-automatic.

Press 2 to switch to the the suppressing constant-firing laser. Hold down the Spacebar to fire the laser. The longer you hold it down, the more damage it does. After a certain threshold, it will instantly kill an enemy.

Keep them at bay for as long as you can, commander. Hacket out.
